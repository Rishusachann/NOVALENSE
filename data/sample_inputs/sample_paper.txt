NOVALENSE studies research originality by combining semantic similarity retrieval, paraphrase-sensitive sentence comparison, and explainable AI-generated text analysis. The framework converts a paper into contextual embeddings, compares it with a scholarly corpus, and reports both document-level and sentence-level evidence. Instead of relying only on exact phrase matches, the system identifies concept overlap and structural rewriting. It also inspects stylometric regularities such as repetition, sentence-length consistency, and token distribution to estimate AI-writing likelihood. The final originality score is produced through weighted fusion of semantic overlap, paraphrase probability, and AI-likelihood.
